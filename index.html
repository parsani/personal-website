I'm a Master's student in Computer Engineering with a focus on Computer Networks at K. N. Toosi University of Technology in Tehran.

Some areas of interest in research for me are: wireless & multimedia networks, reinforcement learning (single- and multi-agent) for network control, Non-Terrestrial Networks (NTN) & satellite networks, & topics in cryptography. The above areas fascinate me, specifically how I can design intelligent, efficient, and robust networked systems.

In addition to my university activities, I am currently serving as a Teaching Assistant for several courses under my supervisor. I am strongly research-oriented with a strong foundation in computer science and mathematics. Moreover, I am also interested in exploring opportunities that combine research with applications, such as industry-based projects involving networking and machine learning.
